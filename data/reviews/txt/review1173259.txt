Dogma

1999

Starring:Linda Fiorentino, Matt Damon,and Ben Affleck.

A reluctant heir of the revered Jesus Christ embarks upon a noble quest.To thwart two renegade angels from returning to Heaven.With the help of a heroic duo,a petulant apostle,and a creative muse.They go to New Jeresy.Without falling victim to the evil Azreal.

A Well Written,Top Notch Comedy that not only has a great cast.But a great story as well.

8/10 My Rating
