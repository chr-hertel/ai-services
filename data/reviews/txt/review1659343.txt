My partner and I were pleasantly surprised by the entertaining elements of this movie, "Books of John." The music was, in our opinion, perfect. The selections made, added an extra artistic flair that made us melt into the movie even more.

To attempt to make a movie that tells a "realistic" story about gay lifestyle, hardships, and relationships - is hard to pull off. Books of John did just that.

Every movie has some sort of message that they want, or attempt to portray to the viewer. Books of John has a "new twist" that leaves you questioning several situations in your own life, and possibly others you've known along the way. 

We won't spoil it, you'll have to find out...
